darts tournament

Dart competitions in pubs often have prizes for the first, second, third and
fourth best throwers. What is the order of play for a darts tournament
involving n throwers that: a) identifies the best k throwers (and their order);
b) has the least number of matches thrown; c) has the least number of games on
each of m dartboards (since tournaments typically run in parallel); d) and is
the most exciting (that is, prize winners should be discovered at the last
possible minute so that everyone stays drinking beer until the end of the
tournament).
